  Weather conditions The country remains hot and dry throughout the year. There is hardly any rainfall. In summers, the days are very hot and the nights are cool. In winters, the days are warm and the nights are very cold. Dust storms are very common in all seasons. In the coastal areas, the temperature remains moderate. Vegetation Very few plants grow in such a harsh environment. Only plants that can survive in hot and dry conditions grow here. These include salt bushes, tamarisk and date palms.

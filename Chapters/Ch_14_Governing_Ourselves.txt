          The Supreme Court of India is the highest court in India. THE STATE GOVERNMENT The state government is responsible for governing the state. Every state has a legislative assembly. It makes laws for the state. Its members are called Members of the Legislative Assembly (MLAs). They are elected by the voters of the state. They serve a five-year term. The Governor is the head of a state. After the Assembly elections, the Governor invites the leader of the majority party to form the government in the state. This leader becomes the Chief Minister.
